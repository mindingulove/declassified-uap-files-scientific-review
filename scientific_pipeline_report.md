- Treat videos as sensor evidence, not self-interpreting proof. Context, sensor geometry, range, platform motion, and environmental correlation are required before kinematic claims.
- Treat annotated images as leads only until unannotated originals are compared.
- Treat scanned historical PDFs as unmined until OCR/manual transcription reaches acceptable quality.
- Separate direct witness testimony, agency summary, and later analytical comment.
